This is Me

My name is Johnny Truong and I am an aspiring technical worker that loves to learn.
Growing up, I found it very difficult to see where I was going and I am sure that is how most people felt. After finishing middle school, I began learning about the world of technology and it why it is a significant factor
in today's world. What caught my attention the most is software, and what many things you can achieve with it. If I were to describe one word about myself, it would be innovative. I indulge in moments where I can build, make, develop, generate, or create anything I want with technology, especially through coding.

What interests me is the world of Star Wars and how it began my journey with technology. Star wars opened my curiosity with hardware and software as the world of the Galactic Republic was filled with advanced technology and how almost every character in the franchise's universe understood technology and how to become a decent to advanced engineer. I love learning and adding to my knowledge, I find computer science, Information Systems, Data Science, and Software Engineering very intriguing and have taken many courses in the CS world and self-taught many CS fundamentals. No matter the difficulty, the motivation I have to learn is robust. Being able to gain new insights and focus on personal and professional growth through intellectual curiosity and understanding the desire for self-improvment is a key characteristic to be engaged in as we will need it to stay ahead in this competitive and vastly changing world.
